3XM Data Analytics Project

Python
Data Analytics
This project applies two Machine learning algorithms to develop a complete business analytics pipeline for 3XM Solutions, a company specialising in premium products for professional photographers. .
Published

November 21, 2025

Overview

The primary objective of this project is to build a supervised ML model that predicts real revenue based on sales data from 2023-present. The project will explore different features, such as product type, country, and shipping method, and how these features impact real revenue. The goal is to be able to utilise this model to enhance operational decision making and marketing strategies by analysing the impact of the features on real revenue.

Results

In Summary, Random Forest outperformed the other models, capturing nonlinear patterns and delivering the best accuracy. Simple linear models had low explanatory power (R²≈0.21–0.29), while Ridge regression (L2) handled multicollinearity with high accuracy (R²≈0.92). Polynomial regression improved the fit over Linear and Ridge models but risks overfitting at higher polynomial degrees.

In conclusion the RF Regressor was the strongest predictive model, explaining 97.68% of the variability in Real Revenue (R2=0.9768). The average prediction error (MAE) was only €14.64. Ridge Regression was selected as the primary regression model for interpretation due to its strong performance (R2≈0.92).

Conclusion

Based on the analysis it is clear that other than total, which is a case of target leakage, the most important predictors of real revenue for 3XM Solutions are cost of shipping and weight, closely followed by day of order and admin order. The use of the RF model without total as well as the ridge regression are the best course of action going forward for 3XM Solutions to utilise ML models for revenue prediction. Given the importance of weight and cost of shipping, 3XM could focus on marketing larger products which provide more revenue in the short term such as folio boxes with prints etc. The lowest feature importance were the country variables, thus there is less need for 3XM to examine countries individually.
